Clock by the Gallon

Clock by the Gallon is an abstract visualization of time through fluid ounces of water. As someone who prioritizes fitness, I made it a goal of mine to drink 1 gallon of water a day, (or 128 fluid ounces.) Through this experimental study, I aim to keep track of how much water I (supposedly) am drinking while simultaneously motivate others to drink their water.

The water level starts at the top of the screen at 12:00:00 AM and lowers by each second of the day, until I drink the full gallon of water. For example, at 4:00 PM I drank 90 fl oz (allegedly).

To create this, each hour, minute, and second are mapped in live time.
